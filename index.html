How Kura works

Deliveries tracked by SMS.
Dashboard updated in real time.

No smartphone, no data bundle, no problem. Kura works on every phone on every network.

💬

SMS Check-in

Driver texts "OK" to a shortcode at each stop. Kura captures the location from the telco cell tower and updates the dashboard instantly. No data, no GPS, no app.

📟

USSD Fallback

If SMS fails, the driver dials a USSD code. A simple menu asks for delivery status via keypad input. Works on any phone, even phones that can’t send SMS.

🗺️

Real-time Dashboard

Operations managers see every delivery on a live map. Status updates (Dispatched → In Transit → Delivered) appear automatically as drivers check in.

🔔

Auto-Customer Notifications

Customers receive SMS updates when their delivery is 30 minutes away, has arrived, or is delayed. No more “Where is my delivery?” calls to your team.
